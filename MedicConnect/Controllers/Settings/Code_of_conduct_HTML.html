Code of Conduct

Our goal is to provide a service that allows users to encourage others by reaching out using our online social currency system. While we respect the ownership of the content that users share, users are responsible for the content that he or she provides. Because of these principles, we do not actively monitor nor censor user content, except in the limited circumstances as described below.

Content Boundaries and Use of Medic Connect

In order to continue providing the Medic Connect service, which gives users the ability to communicate and stay connected with others, there are some limitations on the type of content that we will allow to be published. Such limitations comply with legal requirements, which we believe will make Medic Connect a better user experience for all. Any such breach of the Medic Connect Code of Conduct may result in that user being temporarily or permanently suspended. Medic Connect may update or modify such content boundaries from time to time by posting the changes on this site:

  1. You may not attempt to impersonate another person, business or organization. Users cannot portray others in a manner that does or is intended to mislead, confuse, or deceive.

  2. You may not publish or post other people's private and confidential information without their express consent.

  3. You may be subject to certain limitations, at the discretion of Medic Connect Inc., when posting or referencing sensitive media, such as nudity or violence.

  4. You may not publish or post explicit violent or otherwise graphic content, such as explicit depictions of child abuse, animal abuse, or bodily harm.

  5. You may not post pornographic or sexually explicit content.

  6. You may not publish or post content that is intended to incite others to commit violence, mocks victims of violence, or includes a direct and specific threat of violence to others.

  7. You may not harass or otherwise bully another user. Bullying will result in immediate account suspension.

  8. You may not infringe on the Trademark or Copyright of others. We will respond to valid and complete notices of alleged copyright infringement. Our copyright procedures are set forth in the Terms of Service.

  9. You may not use our service for any unlawful purposes or in furtherance of illegal activities. International users agree to comply with all local laws regarding online conduct and acceptable content.

  10. You may not use the Medic Connect logo unless given express written consent by Medic Connect Inc. to do so. Accounts using any form of Medic Connect branding as part of their profile photos/videos, their Profile description, in any posts, or in a way that falsely implies affiliation with Medic Connect will be immediately suspended.

  11. You may not spam or otherwise abuse other users. Medic Connect strives to protect its users from spam and abuse, and therefore technical and user abuse will not tolerated. Examples of such abuse includes, but are not limited to:

    • Profile Squatting

    • Invitation Spam

    • Buying or selling User Accounts

    • Malware and Phishing

    • Spam

  12. You may not use the Medic Connect service for the purpose of spamming anyone. Some of the factors that we take into account when determining what conduct is considered to be spamming include:

    • Attempting to direct users to your profile

    • Attempting to market your products and/or services

    • Attempting to mislead via tags or hashtags

    • Attempting to increase your own popularity via Likes or Comments by manually creating multiple accounts or otherwise through automated means.

  13. You may be subject to account review and further disciplinary action if:

    • a disproportionate number of users flag your content

    • a disproportionate number of users are blocking your profile;

    • a disproportionate number of users are reporting your profile for spam;

    • you post large numbers of duplicate comments;

    • you post large numbers of unsolicited comments;

    • you "Like" a large number of Medic Connect posts in an attempt to attract attention to a profile;

    • you trade "Likes" or "Comments" with other users;

    • you create or otherwise maintain multiple accounts in order to gain followers;

    • you use or promote third-party sites or services that claim to get you more followers (such as follower trains, sites promising "more followers fast," or any other site or service that offers to automatically add followers to your profile).
