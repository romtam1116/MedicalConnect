Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to Codi Inc. We are very sensitive to the privacy needs of our supporting medical professionals and their patients and is wholly committed to maintaining privacy at the highest level. Codi Inc. does not sell or otherwise share any personal information with anyone. To better understand the way we collect, use, and store information, please review the our Privacy Policy below.

1. User Information

It is your choice whether or not you provide your Information to Codi Inc., but specific user Information is required for Codi Inc. to provide you with services (for example, access to Codi Inc. under a user agreement). User information may be used internally within Codi Inc. in accordance with the Personal Information & Privacy Act (https://www.oipc.bc.ca/guidance-documents/1438). For example, Codi Inc. may use a medical professional's personal and patient information internally to provide our services to our users, process your transactions, to contact you for customer service and billing purposes, and to communicate to you about your account or changes to our service

2. Usage Information

Non-personally identifying information ("Usage Information") comprises information that does not identify any particular user. Examples include: information about which of Codi's features used (or website pages visited), mobile phone, browser type, operating system, or IP address. Your Usage Information may be disclosed internally within Codi Inc. and to third parties. For example, Codi Inc. may disclose Usage Information in order to better understand who and how users use our software (and website) in an attempt to improve our services, and for marketing, advertising, or research purposes.

3. Identifying Purposes

Only information that is directly related to provide our services is collected. Whenever a medical professional provides information to Codi Inc., the client is consenting to the collection, use, and disclosure of that information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and PIPA Regulations and PIPA's recommended "Best Practices".

4. Consent

Codi Inc. will not automatically collect any information that specifically identifies you such as your name, address, or e-mail address. This information is collected only when you voluntarily provide it as part of the Sigh-up process and when creating an account. Patient identifiable information is only collected from medical professionals with their permission and it is never collected passively. Either a medical professional or patient may withdraw their consent for Codi Inc. to collect and/or store identifiable information at any time by notifying the Privacy Officer in writing.

5. Limiting Collection

The information that is collected is strictly limited to what Codi Inc. needs to provide medical consultation and transcription services to our users. However, Codi Inc. reserves the right to disclose your Information under limited circumstances in the event Codi Inc. believes that our software is being used to commit unlawful acts, and/or if disclosure of your user Information is required to comply with applicable laws or regulations, or with a court or administrative order.

6. Limiting Use, Disclosure, & Retention

Only those who "need to know" have access to patient sensitive information. This includes relevant physicians, associated medical personnel, medical transcriptionists, quality control personnel, the Technology Department, and technical support. Patient and Transcribed documentation is stored indefinitely on an encrypted server. All data is backed up daily and a copy is stored securely off-site.

7. Accuracy

We rely on our users to provide us with accurate patient and medical information. We do not perform any verification of patient identifiable information or other information as provided to us by our clients.

8. Safeguards

Codi Inc. has contracts with third party providers to ensure that they also protect the privacy of the information collected and that it is only used for the purposes for which it has been collected. All data is safeguarded with encryption and password protection on all computers, servers, restricted access areas and locked doors and alarms where appropriate. All systems are audited that process, transmit, and/or store patient data. Events to be audited may include, and are not limited to, logins, logouts, file access, deletions and modifications.

Codi Inc. ensures that all workforce members are trained in and understand our security policies and procedures. In addition, all workforce members are trained how to identify, report, and prevent potential security incidents. Codi's long term safeguards include regularly reviewing procedures, reviewing contractor policies on a regular basis, and regularly auditing policies, procedures, training, personnel, and systems.

9. Openness

All information collected is used only for the express purpose of providing medical consult and transcription services to our users.

10. Individual access

Codi Inc. has a designated Privacy Officer. Customers may confirm the accuracy of the information that we have on them on file by contacting the Privacy Officer at 604.618.4420 or privacy@codiapp.com.

11. Challenging compliance

Codi Inc. has a designated Security Officer, who can be contacted at 604.618.4420 or security@codiapp.com. All requests will be acknowledged within one business day of their receipt. Responses will be provided within 30 days. Within that period of time, the information will be released as requested or a written responses as to why the information is not being released will be provided.

If this Privacy Policy changes, the revised policy will be posted on the www.codiapp.com website and on our Mobile App.

12. Effective Date

This Privacy Policy was last updated on January 1st, 2018 and will remain in effect unless changed. Codi Inc. may modify this Privacy Policy at any time, and such modifications shall be effective immediately upon posting of the modified Privacy Policy in App or on our Website.

13. Contacting Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, our practices, or any of our services, please contact info@codiapp.com.
